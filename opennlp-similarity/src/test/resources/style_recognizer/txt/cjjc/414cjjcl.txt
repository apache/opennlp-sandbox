
Association of Spina Bifida & Hydrocephalus ( S. E . Region Office ) ASBAH is the Association for Spina Bifida and Hydrocephalus . We are the national organisation providing information and advice about Spina Bifida and Hydrocephalus to individuals , families and carers via a network of advisers . Our unique services are targeted towards : Support to parents before and around the birth of their baby , or diagnosis of the condition ; Support to the child and family on educational matters ; Specialised information & help on health matters ; Helping young people access services to progress towards control of their lives and much more . more information Isle of Wight Association For Spina Bifida & Hydrocephalus
