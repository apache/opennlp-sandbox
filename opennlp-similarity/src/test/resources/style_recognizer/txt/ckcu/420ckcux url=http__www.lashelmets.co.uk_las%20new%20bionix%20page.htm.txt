
Design : Futuristic and aerodynamic with an aggressive look for competition . Used by Team Liquigas Bianchi . Adjustment : Adjustable size from 54 to 59 . The new CATEYES SYSTEM allows height and tension to be adjusted while on the move . Ventilation : Tested and developed using the wind tunnel , it has exceptional interior climate control thanks to an efficient ventilation system . Thanks to the triple wing directing the air to the vents allows air to be circulated around the head . Excellent ventilation without compromising aerodynamics . Inside : Removable and washable inside padding made of OUTLAST fabric , the most innovative fabric on the market and is used by NASA and has been awarded the Space Technology Certificate . OUTLAST is a heat regulator that keeps the temperature constant . A cotton anti-insect net is combined with the padding . Weight : 280g
